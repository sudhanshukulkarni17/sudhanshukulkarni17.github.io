Study Methodology

Field study is a central part of the curriculum. We try to develop an understanding of issues at the grassroot level, which is facilitated through regular field-visits and interaction with different communities and stakeholders.

Our Research Areas

Data Analytics

Data-based action plan has become new trend for policy planning and implementation. We have expertise who have hands-on experienced in advance tools used for Big data analytics and data visualization. For example STATA, SPSS, VBA, Tableau, QGIS etc.

Data Collection

Our Research Scholars and Masters Students go for compulsory summer field-stay of 9 weeks in rural areas to collect data related to sectors e.g. malnutrition, livelihood, sanitation, energy etc. Also, our team includes expertise of KoBo toolbox and anthropometric data collection.

Product Development

We have developed many nutrient-rich products using local raw materials. These products includes RUSF, RUTF and health-drinks. The cost of these products is cheaper as compared to the products in the existing market. These products can be prepared at household as well.

Strategy Development

Government of India have launched National Nutrition Mission (Poshan Abhiyan) in 2018 to fight malnutrition. As focus of the mission has been shifted from state-level strategy planning to the district level, we have developed Nutrition Action Plan at District and Sub-district level.
